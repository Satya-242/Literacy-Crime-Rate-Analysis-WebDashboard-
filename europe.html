About Europe

Europe is a continent located entirely in the Northern Hemisphere and mostly in the Eastern Hemisphere. It shares its landmass with Asia (together known as Eurasia) and is bordered by the Arctic Ocean to the north, the Atlantic Ocean to the west, and the Mediterranean Sea to the south.

Key Facts:

  • Area: Approximately 10.18 million square kilometers
  • Population: Over 750 million people
  • Number of countries: 44 sovereign nations
  • Largest country: Russia (partly in Asia as well)
  • Languages: English, French, German, Spanish, Russian, Italian, and many others

Major Regions of Europe:

  • Northern Europe (e.g., UK, Sweden, Norway)
  • Southern Europe (e.g., Italy, Greece, Spain)
  • Eastern Europe (e.g., Poland, Ukraine, Romania)
  • Western Europe (e.g., France, Germany, Netherlands)
  • Central Europe (e.g., Austria, Switzerland, Czech Republic)
