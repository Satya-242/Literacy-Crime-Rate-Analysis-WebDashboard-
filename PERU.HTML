Peru is a country in South-america known for its rich cultural heritage and diverse landscapes, from the Andes mountains to the Amazon rainforest. It is home to the ancient Incan city of Machu Picchu, one of the New Seven Wonders of the World. Peru also has a vibrant culinary scene, famous for dishes like ceviche.
