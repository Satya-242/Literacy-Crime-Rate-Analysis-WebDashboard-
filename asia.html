About Asia

Asia is the largest and most populous continent on Earth, located primarily in the Eastern and Northern Hemispheres. It shares its continental landmass with Europe (collectively known as Eurasia) and is bounded by the Pacific Ocean, Indian Ocean, and Arctic Ocean.

Key Facts:

  • Area: About 44.58 million square kilometers
  • Population: Over 4.7 billion people (about 60% of the world's population)
  • Number of countries: 49 countries (plus partially recognized and dependent territories)
  • Largest country: China (by population), Russia (by area, part of both Asia and Europe)
  • Major languages: Mandarin, Hindi, Arabic, Russian, Bengali, Japanese, and many others

Major Regions of Asia:

  • East Asia (e.g., China, Japan, South Korea)
  • South Asia (e.g., India, Pakistan, Bangladesh)
  • Southeast Asia (e.g., Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam)
  • Central Asia (e.g., Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan)
  • West Asia / Middle East (e.g., Saudi Arabia, Iran, Turkey)
  • Northern Asia (mostly Russia)
